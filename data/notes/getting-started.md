Welcome to my digital garden! This is a place where ideas grow and connect.

## What is a Digital Garden?

A digital garden is a collection of notes and ideas that grow and evolve over time. Unlike a blog, which is more like a publication, a digital garden is a living, breathing collection of thoughts.

## Features

- **Knowledge Graph**: Visualize connections between notes
- **Backlinks**: See which notes reference this one
- **Tag Filtering**: Organize notes by topics
- **Search**: Find anything quickly

## Getting Started

1. Browse the [[Knowledge Graph]] to see connections
2. Explore notes by [[tags]]
3. Use the search bar to find specific content

Happy exploring!
